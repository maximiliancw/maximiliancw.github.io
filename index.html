About

Hi, I'm Max - welcome to my personal homepage!

Since 1994, I'm trying to not be bored. My main area of activity is the German state of Saarland.

I am enthusiastic about authenticity and creativity - two of the main ingredients for becoming an entrepreneur. This lead me to abandon academia rather early and to instead start my first own company in 2019: a not-so-innovative digital service provider called INNOVINATI.

Since then, I've co-founded a handful of ventures and joined CISPA Helmholtz Center for Information Security in 2020, where I'm currently working as Head of Startups & Entrepreneurship. While still studying, I worked as an intern for Software AG (2015-2017) and Pricewaterhouse Coopers (2018-2019).
